Workout

Physical activity or exercise can improve your health and reduce the risk of developing several diseases like type 2 diabetes,cancer and cardiovascular disease. Physical activity and exercise can have immediate and long-term health benefits.

Nutrition

A healthy diet throughout life promotes healthy pregnancy outcomes, supports normal growth, development and ageing, helps to maintain a healthy body weight, and reduces the risk of chronic disease leading to overall health and well-being.

Rest

Rest is an important part of a healthy lifestyle for all ages. It rejuvenates your body and mind, regulates your mood, and is linked to learning and memory function. On the other hand, not getting enough rest can negatively affect your mood.
